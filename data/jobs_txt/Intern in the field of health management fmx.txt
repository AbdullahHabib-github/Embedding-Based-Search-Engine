We offer you an exciting and varied internship in the field of occupational health management. As part of your work as an intern (f/m/x), you will support our team in the area of ​​health management and focus on the implementation of measures relating to behavioral ergonomics.


What awaits you?

- You will support our department in the planning, implementation and evaluation of measures relating to health management.
- You independently organize the implementation of the module "Promotion of ergonomic behavior in the workplace and physical and mental resources" of the behavioral ergonomics program in the office areas of the plant.
- You conduct ergonomic training courses at the workplace with employees and take care of ensuring sustainability.
- You are the contact person for employees and managers and, if necessary, also support the implementation of further health-related measures.
- You will gain an insight into all health management topics and occupational medicine activities and will support you in the creation of presentations and communication materials.
- The close cooperation with the internal departments rounds off your exciting area of ​​responsibility.


what do you bring

- Studies in training science, applied movement science, health management, sports science or a comparable course.
- Second half of diploma/bachelor's degree or master's degree.
- Knowledge of the human musculoskeletal system (e.g. training as a physiotherapist, fitness trainer A/B license) or comparable qualification.
- Safe handling of MS Office (Power Point and Excel).
- Fluent knowledge of German.
- Team and communication skills, self-assured demeanor as well as organizational talent and initiative.
