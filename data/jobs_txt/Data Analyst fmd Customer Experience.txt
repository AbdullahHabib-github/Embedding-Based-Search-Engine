What part will you play?

- In your role you support driving customer experience programs (NPS, CXM) in DI Sales with other stakeholders
- Together with partners and G2M guides you will craft the customer journey
- Developing strategies to assess user-centered metrics efficiently and continuously analyzing quantitative research results with confirmed statistical are also part of your task area
- Moreover, you are responsible for consolidating large sets of behavioral user data with user feedback sourced data
- Additionally, you will translate quantitative survey findings and other qualitative sources of feedback across different touch points into practical insights
- Supporting the NPS process and take ownership to be the contact in DI S HQ for internal stakeholders (e.g,. GMS, DI S regions) complete your field of activities


What you need to make real what matters.

- The basis of your success is a Bachelor´s Degree in Business and/or Computer Science or an equivalent qualification
- You bring with first work experience in similar capacity ideally within Sales organization.
- You are a true Data and IT Enthusiast, Enabler for Business and Data Science, open to support change driving the combination of different information and insights to prepare, influence and impact business decisions.
- To ensure impact from data it is critical to seamlessly collaborate cross-functional with various guides from IT, Data Science, and the Business
- Improved data aggregation and visualization skills are essential to transfer the key insights in a relevant and compelling way
- You should bring experience in Sales, experience in NPS and/or other CX related fields is a plus
