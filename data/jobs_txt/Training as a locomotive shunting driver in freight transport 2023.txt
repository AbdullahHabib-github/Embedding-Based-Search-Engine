From September 1st, 2023 we are looking for you for the 3-year apprenticeship as a train driver in freight transport (railway employee in operational service specializing in train drivers / transport) (f/m/d) for DB Cargo AG at the Braunschweig location . The vocational school is in Burgdorf.
What awaits you in your training:


- You drive a diesel locomotive to nearby customers, e.g. B. different companies, and pick up the goods to be transported
- Shunting the freight wagons to the appropriate tracks

- You assemble trains by coupling and uncoupling individual wagons

- You will learn the rules for safe railway operations and your vehicle inside and out

- Checking the functionality of the freight trains and securing the loads are also part of your tasks

- You start and end your working day at your regional marshalling yard


Your profile:


- You have successfully completed school (soon) and are enthusiastic about technology

- You really like working outside in the fresh air

- Even in challenging situations you keep calm and keep track of things

- You like to take responsibility and make decisions

- Reliability and resilience characterize you

- You can imagine working in shifts and also on weekends and public holidays
