What you move with us:

As one of our many special services, we offer our customers a pick-up service. The items from our range can be easily ordered online. You then make the goods available for collection at your REWE Koerner oHG at the desired time:

- You support sales, take care of the stocking of goods, price labeling and our customers are well advised with you.
