Job description:

- Your main task is the coordination of the functional safety activities in the project according to ISO 26262.
- You create the safety plan, the safety analysis (FMEA, FMEDA, FTA, reliability), the safety concept and the safety case.
- In your position as Functional Safety Engineer you are responsible for supporting the development team in the implementation of safety concepts at system, hardware and software level.
- In addition, you are the direct contact for customers to coordinate product requirements with regard to functional safety.
- Your area of ​​responsibility will be rounded off with support in the projects and contact persons in questions of functional safety

Your profile:

- You have a degree in engineering or comparable training.
- In addition, you can show several years of professional experience in the areas of embedded systems, hardware design or software design in the automotive industry.
- You have very good knowledge of requirements management and documentation.
- Fluent knowledge of the English and German language and willingness to travel round off your profile.
