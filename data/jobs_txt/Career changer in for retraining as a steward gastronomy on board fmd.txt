As of June 1st, 2023, we are looking for you as a career changer for retraining as a steward in gastronomy on board (f/m/d) for DB Fernverkehr AG at the Stuttgart location.
What to expect from the retraining:


- The retraining takes place on a full-time basis and consists of theoretical and practical sections

- The duration of the retraining is about 4 weeks and ends with a final exam

- The theoretical lessons and your practical assignments take place at the Stuttgart location
- The training class consists of approx. 10 participants


If you would then like to develop yourself further, then after gaining professional experience, you can qualify as a restaurant manager.


After your retraining, you will take on the following activities:


- You make every journey in the ICE and Intercity a first-class experience for our guests

- As an attentive host, you ensure a pleasant atmosphere on board and help with boarding and disembarking

- You are responsible for preparing food and drinks in the on-board restaurant

- In 1st and 2nd class you serve our gastronomic offer at your seat

- You are available to our guests as a competent contact person with questions and advice

- You offer a high quality of service and make sure that the guests feel completely comfortable


Your profile:


- Ideally, you have successfully completed training in a service-oriented profession such as a restaurant or hotel specialist, alternatively you have already gained professional experience in the catering or hotel industry

- As an active seller, you can achieve sales targets with additional or alternative offers

- Due to your strong teamwork and communication skills, dealing with people suits you, in particular the sovereign resolution of conflict situations

- You are open to digital means of communication - tablets, apps and co. are welcome with you

- It goes without saying that you will be willing to spend the night away from home and work irregular shifts and alternating service

- You are fluent in spoken and written German, and you can also converse with your guests in English


Applications from already trained stewards are also welcome.
