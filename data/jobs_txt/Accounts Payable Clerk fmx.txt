The Accounting and Services department is responsible for and operates the accounting of the industrial business of the BMW Group in Europe and offers company-wide services at the Munich location. We are accounts payable, the competence center for transaction processes, for closing and reporting in the sub-ledgers of the industrial business.

What to expect

- You will monitor the creditor supplier accounts with regard to the timely payment of our liabilities.
- You specify the parameters for the automatic payment runs.
- Another focus of your work is the management of accounts receivable from creditors with debit balances, which includes the entire spectrum from the technical offsetting of accounts receivable through to dunning procedures.
- You take over the communication with our suppliers as well as internal partners such as purchasing, returns or invoice verification.


What are you bringing with you?

- Completed training in the commercial field with an additional business qualification or a comparable qualification.
- Accounting experience, ideally Accounts Payable.
- Sound knowledge of MS Office and SAP.
- Proficient in German and English.
