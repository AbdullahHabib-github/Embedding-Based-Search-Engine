Apprenticeship as a retail clerk (m/f/d) - 2023

What are you making of yourself?

Become a confidant. During the training you will master a variety of tasks, because you and your team are responsible for the smooth running of business in the store. The focus is on the customer: You are the contact person and important advisor for our customers. You also operate the tills, do the accounting and are responsible for data analysis. In addition to sales, you will work in purchasing and warehousing. You will also be involved in the design of the product range and in marketing campaigns.

- You are involved in the design of the assortment and the structure of the goods
- You master everything to do with the goods - from purchasing to sales to marketing
- You ensure compliance with and control of hygiene regulations

What should you bring with you?

- Enjoying customer contact
- Team spirit
- sense of responsibility
- Interest in business contexts
- Pleasant appearance, well-groomed appearance
