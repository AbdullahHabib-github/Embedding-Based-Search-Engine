On June 15th, 2023 we are looking for you as a career changer for retraining as a shunting attendant (f/m/d) for DB Cargo AG at the Dingolfing location. In the 3-month in-house retraining at the training location in Munich, we prepare you for your job entry.
What to expect from the retraining:


- The retraining takes place full-time (39h/week) and consists of theoretical and practical teaching sections

- The training classes consist of approx. 12 participants
- As part of the retraining as a shunting attendant, you will simultaneously acquire the qualifications as a shunting worker and authorized brake tester


After your retraining, you will take on the following activities:


- You are responsible for shunting work when breaking up and forming trains

- You will also be responsible for preparing and dispatching trains, as well as technical exit checks and brake tests

- The visual inspection of wagons, customs clearance and processing the trains in the IT systems are also part of your duties

- You are in constant contact with locomotive shunting drivers, dispatchers, points wardens and other parties involved

- During shunting trips, you reliably support the locomotive shunting drivers in observing the route


Your profile:


- Ideally, you have successfully completed school
- Your medical suitability is a prerequisite for admission and will be examined by a company doctor as part of the selection process

- Even beyond the internal qualification, you show commitment to continuous further training

- You always work conscientiously and look forward to working in a team

- You don't mind working outdoors, as well as shift work and alternating service (also on Sundays and public holidays).

- Good knowledge of spoken and written German (at least B2 level) rounds off your profile and ideally you also bring a valid category B driving license with you
