Apprenticeship as a salesperson - specializing in bakery (m/f/d) - 2023

What are you making of yourself?

Become a pleasure ambassador. As a seller, you and your team are the driving force behind every purchase. The focus is on service readiness: With your friendly advice, you make shopping easier for everyone and increase general customer satisfaction with your know-how. You will also take care of the smooth process of receiving goods, price labeling and the sales-promoting presentation of the baked goods. You serve the tills and do the accounting.
At the same time, you inform customers about the goods and shine with your expertise and your friendliness.

• You master everything to do with bread and baked goods - from purchasing to sales to marketing
• You will learn how to deal with raw materials and bakery products and how they are processed in different ways

What should you bring with you?

- Enjoying customer contact
- Team spirit
- Empathy
- Pleasant appearance, well-groomed appearance
- Good expressiveness

duration and expiration

Training to become a salesperson lasts two years. Under certain conditions, a reduction is possible. In addition to the vocational school, you will attend seminars and receive well-founded exam preparation, which only EDEKA offers in this form and which provides you with the relevant knowledge for practice.
