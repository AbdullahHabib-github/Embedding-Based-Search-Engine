Your new area of ​​responsibility – challenging and future-oriented

- From July 1, 2023 you will start your master's degree at Steinbeis University and your job at Siemens Mobility GmbH in Munich or Erlangen.
- Through your study projects in our company, you will gain extensive insights into the structure of HR work in a large corporation.
- You will work in the field of personnel support and consulting and be the contact person for employees and managers.
- You will acquire knowledge of all HR issues, such as recruitment, terminations and personnel development.
- You will also gain valuable experience by working on various HR projects
- During your studies, you will be financially supported by Siemens Mobility GmbH with a monthly study grant, and tuition fees and travel expenses will also be covered.


Your qualifications – well-founded and adequate

- You have completed your bachelor's degree in business administration, education, psychology or a comparable subject with above-average success.
- You have already gained your first practical experience as a working student or intern, ideally in the HR area.
- You are an open and communicative personality who enjoys working in a team and is not afraid to speak in front of larger groups.
- We require very good knowledge of German and English.
