Construction machine operator (m/f/d)
HERE YOU WILL WORK !

Are you looking for a career change, are you looking for a varied job and don't want to give up the security of a reliable and trustworthy employer? Then we can offer you the position you are looking for here: as a construction machine operator (m/f/d) at our customer's location in Kringelsdorf.

Tasks, competencies and responsibilities
- You operate and maintain the construction machine (wheel loader, excavator, roller)
- You are responsible for the loading and stockpiling of bulk goods
- Your area of ​​responsibility includes the organization of the storage area
- As a construction machine operator, you weigh and control the incoming and outgoing quantities

Technical requirements
- You have a tractor T and a truck driver's license
- You already have some professional experience as a construction machine operator
- Knowledge of weighing and PC is advantageous
- You work independently and in a structured manner
