Logistic Support Engineer (m/f/d)

Location: Flintbek
Employment type(s): full-time

Tasks, competencies and responsibilities
As a Logistic Support Engineer (m/f/d) you will be responsible for the following tasks:


- You analyze the technical and logistical specifications of our customers in relation to LSA and LCC requirements
- You carry out LSA analyzes such as FMECA, RCM, MTA and LORA for our products
- You evaluate the analysis results taking into account customer-specific maintenance concepts and have an influence on our products
- You determine spare parts suggestions and define necessary maintenance measures, special tools and training requirements with other departments
- You take over acceptance and quality assurance for deliveries from subcontractors
- You maintain the LSA and ILS data and thus lay the foundation for subsequent ILS service provision - e.g. our technical logistic documentation

Technical requirements
as a Logistic Support Engineer (m/f/d) you ideally have the following qualifications:


- You have a qualified technical background (completed studies or further training as a technician)
- You can show professional experience in the areas of LSA and LCC and, if possible, in the area of ​​logistical service provision in the army/navy/air force
- You have knowledge of the practical application of the data elements MIL-Std-1388, ASD S2000M and ASD S3000L
- You have very good knowledge of SQL database systems and Office (Excel, Word etc.)
- Knowledge of Autodesk Inventor and DWG viewers such as Navisworks is also desirable
- Good written and spoken English skills and the willingness to travel on business round off your profile
