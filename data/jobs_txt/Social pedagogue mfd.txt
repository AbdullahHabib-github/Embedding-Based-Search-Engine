Who we are:

KINDERLAND-Sachsen e.V. designs needs-based offers to support children, young people and families throughout Saxony.

In our residential group "Go5" live in two age-homogenous groups of 5 children and young people between the ages of 3 and 18 years. Girls and boys whose living conditions in their family of origin do not currently guarantee adequate care and development can find a temporary home here. We want to relieve the parents and their children during this time and create the opportunity to change existing problem situations in order to make living together in the family possible again or to accompany the young people to independence.
