Your new area of ​​responsibility – challenging and future-oriented

At our Dortmund-Bornstrasse location, we operate a state-of-the-art service depot where rail vehicles of various types are maintained. For the maintenance and commissioning of rail vehicles, we are looking for a sub-project manager for work preparation / production-accompanying quality assurance (AV/QS) (m/f/d) as soon as possible.

- In your new role, you will lead, instruct and guide the employees assigned to the team.
- You are responsible for the planning, preparation, monitoring, implementation and further development of preventive and corrective maintenance measures.
- You are responsible for checking all documents created to document the maintenance work carried out
- When preparing offers, you will be involved in all aspects of work preparation and quality assurance during production.
- We offer you a wide range of development and training opportunities and the opportunity for a long-term, promising job in rail vehicle maintenance.


Your qualifications – well-founded and adequate

- You have completed a technical bachelor's degree.
- In addition, you have already gained initial experience in managing maintenance projects for rail vehicles.
- You have very good technical knowledge of rail vehicles, especially in their maintenance.
- You also convince with initial experience in the technical management of employees.
- You have know-how in the IT tools commonly used for maintenance and ideally know the EU VO 2019/779.
- You also have a car driver's license.
