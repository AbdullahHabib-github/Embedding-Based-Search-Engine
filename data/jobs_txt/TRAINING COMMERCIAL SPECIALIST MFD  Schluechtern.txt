YOUR TRAINING AT JYSK

We not only train you to be a sales professional who inspires our customers and ensures unforgettable shopping experiences, but also to become a successful manager.

You learn the practical side in the store and the theory in the training center for retail trade in Lower Saxony (BZE) - we take over the accommodation, meals as well as the travel and accommodation costs during the block lesson.

In monthly, personal meetings, you will receive feedback on your progress, potential and training-related topics.

As a future manager, you have the opportunity to manage a store as a substitute during your training.

We make sure that you can grow professionally and personally with us. You will be in contact with other trainees and great mentors.
